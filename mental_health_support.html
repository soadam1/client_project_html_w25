Mental Health Support at UMSI

Understanding the Importance of Mental Health

Graduate school can be a demanding time with academic, social, and personal pressures. Acknowledging the importance of mental health is the first step toward thriving academically and personally. Prioritizing mental wellness can enhance your focus, creativity, and resilience, helping you navigate both challenges and opportunities effectively.

Navigating Stress and Anxiety

  • Recognize and Acknowledge: It's normal to feel stressed or anxious during this phase. Recognizing these feelings can help you approach them proactively.
  • Mindfulness and Meditation: Engage in mindfulness practices or meditation to help calm your mind and improve concentration.
  • Time Management: Develop a realistic schedule that balances study, work, and leisure.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

The University Health & Counseling (UHC) provides individual peer counseling with a goal of creating positive pathways for students' mental health.
